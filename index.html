  • A Scrumptious Low-Calorie Diet Cookbook: Scrumptious Low-Calorie Recipes, Breakfasts to Desserts

    If you’re looking for lower-calorie recipes of your favorite foods, this cookbook is for you! This cookbook contains low-calorie recipes for: Breakfasts and Beverages; Main Dishes, Sides, Salads; Soups, Stews, Chilis; Snacks and Desserts That’s over 150 low-calorie recipes to choose from! Most (though not all) are quick and easy to make! These tasty recipes include biscuits and muffins; casseroles, meatloaf, and pizza; salad dressings and potato salads; soups, stews, and chilis; corn chips and dips; shakes and smoothies; cakes, pies, and cookies. Recipes include information on calories, fat, and fiber (most contain protein information, too). Please note: Many recipes contain good carbs such as milk, fruits, and vegetables. This is not a low-carb cookbook. Recipes are for lower calorie versions of full-calorie foods. If you wish to lower your calorie count, there are recipes to use. This is not a recipe book for those looking for zero (or near-zero) calorie recipes. Some of the recipes call for artificial sweeteners or canned foods for convenience.
